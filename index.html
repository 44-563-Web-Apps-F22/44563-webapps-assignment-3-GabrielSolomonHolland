The Nelson Atkins Museum of Art

The Nelson Atkins is a great museum in Kansas City, Missouri. Must see exhibits include the Egypt section, which contains a real mummy! There is also the Africa section, which is classified differently than Egypt even though Egypt is in Africa. I mean I guess separating those makes some sense because of vastly different cultures and time periods but also, really guys? It seems to broadly generalize the cultures of sub-saharah Africa.

Airport and instructions to get there

The airport is "Kansas City International Airport" (MSI)

  1. Get in your car
  2. Insert your key and turn it
  3. Verify that you have enough gas
  4. Open your maps tool of choice
    1. Why would any generation Z have directions memorized
  5. Once in your car, navigate out of the parking lot
  6. Follow road signs to i-29 S/US 71 S
    1. There are signs posted periodically throughout the drivable area of the airport
  7. Merge onto i-29 / US-71
    1. Stay on this road for 17 Miles
  8. Keep left to merge onto US-71 S
    1. Continue for an additional 4 miles
  9. Exit towards Emanuel Cleaver II Road
  10. Go right at the fork to merge onto Emanuel Cleaver II Road
  11. Continue for 1.5 miles
  12. Turn Right onto Oak Street
  13. You have arrived at your destination
